The Competition

The America's Cup is globally regarded as the Formula 1 of international yacht racing, first started in 1851 the Schooner America crossed the atlantic. The America's Cup predates the modern Olympics and several sport championships worldwide, in which all nations compete under the Deed of Gift.

Challenges

Each yacht combines technical and engineering challenges in material sciences, aerodynamics and hydrodynamics. Each team demonstrates these innovations by blending expertise from not only sailing, but also from other disciplines such as cycling, rowing and more.
