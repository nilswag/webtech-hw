Emirates Team New Zealand

Emirates Team New Zealand is New Zealand's elite America's Cup sailing team, representing the Royal New Zealand Yacht Squadron and based in Auckland. Founded in 1993, the team rose to international prominence with its historic victory at the 1995 America's Cup, becoming the first team from outside the United States to win sailing's oldest trophy. They successfully defended the Cup in 2000 and have since added further victories in 2017, 2021, and 2024 — establishing themselves as the most successful team of the modern foiling era.

A Legacy of Excellence

Emirates Team New Zealand has built its reputation on innovation, teamwork, and resilience. Guided over the years by legendary figures such as Sir Peter Blake, Russell Coutts, and Peter Burling, the team has consistently pushed the boundaries of yacht design and performance. Their 2017 campaign introduced a revolutionary cycling-powered grinding system aboard their AC50 catamaran, a bold innovation that helped secure a dominant victory. In the AC75 era, they continued to lead the fleet with cutting-edge foiling technology and meticulous performance development.

Competing at the Highest Level

The team competes in the America's Cup, sailing's most prestigious and historic competition dating back to 1851. As multi-time champions, Emirates Team New Zealand currently holds the role of Defender for the 2027 America's Cup, scheduled to take place in Naples, Italy.

Innovation and Kiwi Ingenuity

Known for combining world-class sailors with leading marine engineers and designers, Emirates Team New Zealand blends sporting excellence with advanced technology. From the iconic “Black Magic” yachts of the 1990s to today's high-speed foiling AC75s, the team continues to redefine what is possible in competitive sailing. Emirates Team New Zealand represents more than victories on the water — it represents determination, innovation, and the spirit of Aotearoa on the global stage.

A Culture Built on Resilience

Throughout its history, the team has faced both triumph and heartbreak — from dominant victories to dramatic comeback defeats. Each campaign has strengthened the organisation's culture of perseverance and continuous improvement. Setbacks have consistently been met with smarter design, sharper execution, and renewed determination to return stronger.

Leadership and Vision

Under the leadership of CEO Grant Dalton and a world-class sailing and design group, Emirates Team New Zealand operates at the intersection of elite sport and high-performance engineering. The team's structure integrates sailors, designers, shore crew, and technology specialists into a single performance unit, ensuring that innovation moves seamlessly from concept to racecourse.

Representing Aotearoa on the World Stage

More than a professional sailing team, Emirates Team New Zealand represents the pride and ambition of New Zealand. Their campaigns unite fans across the country and inspire the next generation of sailors, engineers, and innovators. Every launch, every regatta, and every defense of the Cup carries the silver fern and the spirit of Aotearoa onto the global stage.
